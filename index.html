Food Science

Nutrition built on
real science

NutriTrack isn't just another calorie counter. It applies validated nutritional science including Harris-Benedict equations, AMDR guidelines, and WHO fiber recommendations to calculate what your body actually needs.

Every user receives unique targets derived from body weight, height, age, sex, and physical activity level. As your body changes, your targets update automatically.

Meal plans are built around macro balance, micronutrient density, and food synergy ensuring you don't just hit numbers, but genuinely nourish your body.
